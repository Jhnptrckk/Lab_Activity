UNITED STATES OF AMERICA

It is home to a wide variety of ecosystems, including the Great Plains, wetlands, and coastal regions. The country has a rich natural heritage, including national parks like Yellowstone and the Grand Canyon, and it plays a major role in global environmental issues.

The U.S. is one of the largest producers of renewable energy, particularly wind and solar, while also being a major consumer of fossil fuels. Its natural resources include abundant minerals, oil, and water, with the Great Lakes forming one of the largest freshwater systems in the world.
